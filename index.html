Our Story

A Passion Baked Into Every Day

At Jacks Country Bakehouse Cafe, every morning begins before the sun rises — with flour-dusted hands, warm ovens, and a deep passion baked into everything we create.

Nestled in Sale, Victoria, we're where friends reconnect over our famous Flat White, and every bite of our Crispy Confit Chicken tells a story of love.

From stunning custom cakes to our Teddy Bear Cookies and Braided Biscuits — everything is handcrafted fresh daily. No shortcuts. Just honest country baking.
